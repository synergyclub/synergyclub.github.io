General info on Synergy

How did the idea for Synergy come about

Mr. Ward from Strath Haven Middle School addresses environmental issues and educational challenges by forming a club for students. They will create a portable robot powered by solar panels, enabling them to charge their Chromebooks anywhere, ensuring better focus and learning opportunities while promoting the use of renewable energy.

What made you choose the design you did?

First and foremost the first Synergy was not even called Synergy. It was called a Rick-Roll. No not that kind. It was named after the person who made it, Ricky. And well it rolled, so Rick-Roll!
