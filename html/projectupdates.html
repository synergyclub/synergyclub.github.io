Challenges

The main issue with Synergy 1 and Synergy 2 is that they weigh quite a lot, both of them have car batteries in them and weigh around 30 pounds each so they are quite heavy. The way we are overcoming this challenge is with Synergy 3. Synergy 3 is going to have the components of a car battery inside so it will not have such issues.

Where did the PUC idea come from?

We picked the PUC idea because the other two Synergys are too big and heavy, which makes them next to impossible to lug around, especially when a stairwell has to get involved. So, in order to make it easier we created the PUC idea.
