Welcome to Synergy

Revolutionizing the way you power your computers

Past Technology

Mr. Ward from Strath Haven Middle School tackles environmental issues and addresses educational challenges by forming a club. This club creates a portable robot powered by solar panels, enabling them to charge their Chromebooks anywhere.

Present Technology

Synergy is conducting battery research and developing a website to share our designs. We aim to collaborate with the community and connect with teachers in both middle and high schools, fostering a collective exchange of ideas and insights as we prepare to showcase our work.

Future Technology

At Strath Haven Middle School, students face battery issues with their Chromebooks. The Synergy club is designing portable laptop chargers called Rick Rolls (RR) to address this. The team aims to create smaller, lighter, 3D-printable chargers with greater capacity, enabling efficient charging for multiple devices in every classroom.
